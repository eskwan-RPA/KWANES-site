01 About

I'm a cloud computing educator and curriculum leader specialising in AWS architecture. In 2021 I launched the Specialist Diploma in Cloud Computing and Intelligent Services Management at Nanyang Polytechnic, and served as its Course Manager from 2021 to 2025.

As Module Leader for Cloud Architecture and Implementation, I taught AWS cloud architecture best practices and guided learners to build cloud-based web applications with a focus on scalability, security, and sustainability. In 2023 I oversaw the diploma's renewal, refreshing the curriculum to incorporate new sustainability elements and the use of Amazon Q across modules.
